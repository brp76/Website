Product Info

Hats for Cats offers a wide variety of options for our customers. Our hats are made from the finest materials, ranging from smooth silk to industrial carbon fiber. One thing all our hats have in common is that they are made with love and a little bit of cat fur.

Here at Hats for Cats, we offer products that vary in both quantity and quality:

  • Quantity
    • We will offer packs with different numbers of hats. You can purchase a singular hat if you wish. Maybe a pack of 3 hats suits you better. If you are a crazy cat lady or a fashionista you can even buy a pack of 10 cat hats.
  • Quality
    • We also offer a variety of specialty hats for a little extra cost. Spice up your cat's hat with global influences such as a French Beret. Equip your cat with protective headgear such as a Football Helmet.
