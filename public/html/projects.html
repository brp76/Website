Main Focus: `Display Driver`

The `Display Driver` is responsible for controlling the flow of data to and from the classes which provide the display drawings. This will interact heavily with the `Driver` to maintain proper flow of information and, thus, the game.

Randomly displays a group of three LEDs on the NeoPixel Ring. The user must move the analog stick connected to the Arduino to control a pixel of their own on the ring. If the user is able to hit the group of LEDs with their own pixel within the limited timeframe, they earn a point for that level. There are multiple "attacks" per level and five levels that become increasingly faster as the game progresses.
