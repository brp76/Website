My Future as a Professional Engineer

      Becoming a Professional Engineer is an extremely valuable accomplishment because of the important role they hold in society. The process to become a Professional Engineer involves a four-year college degree, four years of work under a licensed Professional Engineer, two competency exams and earning a license from a state's licensure board. They must undergo this process to become trustworthy individuals to clients, because of the responsibility they hold. Professional Engineers must make ethical and effective decisions because their work can have significant impacts on the safety and lives of the public. The technologies that Professional Engineers develop is put to use every day by many people, so it is vital that their safety is top notch.
