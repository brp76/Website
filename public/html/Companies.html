What types of companies hire engineers?

  1. Google

    Google is a company that mainly specializes in Internet-related services and products. It was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin in 1998. Their mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful.

    Currently, the Google Search engine is the most widely-used worldwide. An interesting technology they are developing is their self-driving car. To read more about this technology click here. The self-driving car is an amazing technology that aims to improve the safety of roadways.

    Google's headquarters is located in Mountain View, CA in the United States, but it also has many locations around the world such as Belgium, Canada, Czech Republic, France, Germany, Argentina, Egypt, Australia, China, and many more. There is also a convenient location in Pittsburgh. On average, a Software Engineer at Google makes about $130,000 anually.

    • Google Search
    • Google Chrome

    • Gmail
    • Android
    • Google driverless car

    Types of services offered:
    • Search tools
    • Communications
    • Advertising
    • Robotics

  2. Amazon
    3.



    Amazon is a company that mainly operates as an online retailer but also offers other electronic services. It was founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos. Their mission is to be Earth's most customer-centric company, where customers can find and discover anything they might want to buy online, and endeavors to offer its customers the lowest possible prices.

    They are currently the largest Internet-based company in the United States. As part of their online retail service, they are developing delivery drones to transport packages to customers. The other technologies they offer are mainly consumer electronics such as the Amazon Kindle, which is an e-book reader.

    Their headquarters are in Seattle, WA in the United States, but they also have many international locations in places such as the United Kingdom, Germany, Canada, China, India, France, Brazil, and many more. The closest location to Pittsburgh it the one in Harrisburg. Software Engineers at Amazon make about $100,000 a year.

    Types of products:

    • Amazon.com
    • Amazon Kindle
    • Amazon Instant Video

    Types of services offered:
    • Online retail
    • Consumer electronics
    • Entertainment
